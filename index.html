Katharina Lutz is a graphic designer and printmaking enthusiast from Bavaria, Germany, with a deep passion for typography and analogue processes. Her practice explores the decelerating potential and continued relevance of analogue printmaking in today’s fast-paced, post-digital world.
She works with language and typography in abstract, non-conventional forms to reflect on themes such as the relationship between body and mind, thought processes, complex emotions, and human nature.
